Hi! I'm Jason, and welcome to my website. Here, you'll learn about me, my previous internships and projects, and some interesting fun facts!

About Me
My name is Jason, and I'm a first-year graduate student pursuing a Master of Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. I completed my undergrad at UIUC in May 2023 and graduated with a BS in Bioengineering and Minor in Computer Science. Previously, I have worked at McDonald's as a SWE Intern and Argonne National Laboratory as a Machine Learning Intern. I'm excited to contribute to innovative projects and continue learning in the dynamic world of tech, and I hope to work as a fulltime software engineer after graduating!

Career
Long story short, I realized the pre-medical track was not for me, and I discovered that I enjoyed the programming aspects of all my Bioengineering courses. This led me to pursue a Minor and aforementioned internship opportunities in the field of Computer Science. Currently, my interests lie in desktop application development and web development (primarily backend), and combining that with the power of AI/ML. However, as I am in the beginning of my "new" career, I am extremely open to learning and pursuing new opportunities outside of those previously mentioned.

Hobbies
I love listening to music, watching TV shows, playing video games, and doing things with my family! My favorite genres are combinations of Chillstep, EDM, Pop, and Indie. Some of my favorite shows are Game of Thrones, The Bear, and Invincible. My current favorite video games are League of Legends and Dark and Darker. Lastly, I love doing things with my family, whether that be eating out, visiting and traveling to places, or just simply spending time together. I'll end this section with a couple fun facts: I am triplet! I am also an avid dog-lover (although I don't have one...), and I want a Bernese Mountain Dog in the future.
