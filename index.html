Another significant aspect of my role involved optimizing and refactoring existing machine learning models. Through these efforts, I managed to enhance the performance, accuracy, and overall usability of these models. By driving this project, I gained valuable experience in the practical application of AI and ML techniques to tackle real-world environmental issues.
